1Install the app on your phone 安装应用到手机

The app works in any web browser, but installing it on your home screen makes it open like a normal app — faster, full-screen, and works offline once installed.

应用可在任何浏览器中使用。安装到主屏幕后可像普通应用一样使用——更快、全屏、安装后可离线使用。

On Android phones · 安卓手机

  1. Open Chrome and go to the app's web address.用 Chrome 浏览器打开应用网址。
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu at the top right.点击右上角的 ⋮ 菜单。
  3. Tap "Install app" or "Add to Home screen".点击 "安装应用" 或 "添加到主屏幕"。
  4. The icon will appear on your home screen. Tap to open.应用图标出现在主屏幕上。点击图标打开。

On iPhone · 苹果手机

  1. Open Safari (not Chrome) and go to the app's web address.使用 Safari 浏览器（不是 Chrome）打开应用网址。
  2. Tap the Share button (square with up-arrow) at the bottom.点击底部的 分享按钮（方框带向上箭头）。
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen".向下滑动，点击 "添加到主屏幕"。
  4. Tap Add in the top right.点击右上角的 添加。
Tip · 提示: Once installed, the app works even without internet, except for the first time you open it. Save the icon to your home screen for quick access in the orchard.

安装后即使没有网络也可使用（首次打开除外）。把图标保存到主屏幕，方便在果园中快速使用。

2Open the app 打开应用

Tap the green leaf icon on your home screen. The app loads in seconds.

点击主屏幕上的绿色叶子图标。应用几秒内即可打开。

When the app opens, you'll see today's date already filled in, and the planner ready to go. You don't need to log in or create an account.

应用打开后，今天的日期已自动填入，规划器即可使用。不需要登录或注册账号。

3Set your tree details 选择树的资料

The app needs four pieces of information to give you the right dose:

应用需要四项资料来计算正确用量：

① Clone · 品种

Pick the variety of your durian tree from the dropdown — e.g. Musang King (猫山王 / D197), Black Thorn (黑刺 / D200), Monthong (金枕 / D159).
从下拉菜单选择品种——例如 猫山王 D197、黑刺 D200、金枕 D159。
Each clone has a slightly different growing pace. The stage durations and harvest date adjust automatically.
每个品种的生长速度略有不同。各阶段时长和预计收成日期会自动调整。

② Date · 日期

Today's date is filled in by default. Change it only if you want to plan for a future date or look back at a past stage.
默认填入今天日期。如需规划未来或查看过去阶段，可手动修改。

③ Mode · 施肥方式

Choose how you want to fertilize:

  • 🌿 Organic 有机 — pure organic compost, granule, and foliar products.纯有机：堆肥、粒肥和叶面肥。
  • ⚗️ SKO 综合 — combination of organic + chemical. Used in rotation for cost balance.综合方案：有机与化肥轮换施用，平衡成本。

The Chemical-only mode is shown alongside for reference — you can compare doses across modes.
纯化肥方案会一并显示，方便对比各方案的用量。

④ Tree Age · 树龄

Pick the tree's age band. The app supports five tiers:
选择树龄段。应用支持五个等级：
Tier · 等级Size · 树形
0–1 yr 0–1年3–5 ft seedling 3–5尺幼苗
1–2 yr 1–2年Young tree 幼龄树
3–4 yr 3–4年Establishing tree 壮龄树
5+ yr 5年以上Large tree, starts bearing 大型树，开始结果
10+ yr 10年以上Mature large tree 成熟大树
Important · 重要: Trees younger than 5 years are not at bearing age. The app will show flowering stages, but do not force-induce flowering before the tree is ready. Minimum 5 years.

未满5年的树尚未达到结果期。应用会显示开花阶段，但请勿在树未准备好前强制催花。最低需 5 年。

4Read the recommendation 查看施肥建议

Once your four inputs are set, scroll down to see the current stage card. It shows:

设置好四项资料后，向下滑动查看当前阶段卡片，包括：

  1. Current stage — which of the 9 growth stages your tree is in today.目前阶段——树今天处于九个生长阶段中的哪一个。
  2. Product list — what to apply, in the order they should be used.产品清单——按使用顺序列出。
  3. Dose — how much per tree (kg/tree), automatically scaled to your tree's age tier.用量——每棵树多少公斤，按树龄自动换算。
  4. Frequency — how often to apply (e.g. monthly, weekly, every 2–3 days).频率——多久施一次（如每月、每周、每 2–3 天）。
  5. Type chip — tells you what kind of product: 堆肥 Compost 粒肥 Granule 叶面 Foliar 化肥 Chemical.类型标签——告诉你这是什么类别的肥料。
Dose example · 用量举例:
For a 10+ year Musang King in the Vegetative stage with the Organic mode, you might see:
Base Compost EXTRA · 20–25 kg/tree · Monthly (Rotation 1/3).

10年以上的猫山王在营养生长期、有机方案下，可能会看到：
超级堆肥 · 20–25 公斤/树 · 每月一次（轮换 1/3）。

Showing other stages · 查看其他阶段

Tap the stage numbers (1–9) at the top to jump to any stage. You can also toggle "Past stages" or "Upcoming stages" to plan ahead or review what's been done.

点击上方的阶段编号（1–9）跳转到任何阶段。也可切换"已过阶段"或"即将到来的阶段"来回顾或预先规划。

5The 9 growth stages 九个生长阶段

Durian trees go through a yearly cycle. Each stage needs a different nutrient mix:

榴莲树每年经历以下九个阶段，每个阶段所需养分不同：

#Stage阶段What's happening · 此时
1Vegetative营养生长期Tree builds canopy · 培育树冠
2Pre-Flowering催花期Trigger flower buds · 诱导花芽
3Full Bloom开花期Flowers open · 花朵盛开
4Fruit Set坐果初期Young fruits form · 幼果形成
51st Culling第一波落果Natural fruit drop · 自然落果
62nd Culling第二波落果Second drop · 二次落果
7Expansion膨果期Fruits grow fast · 果实膨大
8Maturation成熟落果Fruits ripen and drop · 果实成熟落地
9Post-harvest采收后恢复Tree recovers strength · 树势恢复
During Full Bloom (Stage 3) · 开花期间（第3阶段）: Do not apply granular fertilizer or pesticides. Use only foliar sprays. Protect nocturnal pollinators (bats, moths).

开花期间不要施粒状肥料或农药。仅使用叶面肥。保护夜间授粉者（蝙蝠、蛾）。

6Practical tips 实用建议

① Apply early in the morning or late afternoon · 清晨或傍晚施肥

Avoid midday heat. The tree absorbs nutrients better when it's cool.

避免中午高温。低温时树木吸收养分更好。

② Foliar sprays at 1:500 dilution · 叶面肥 1:500 倍稀释

When the app says "Foliar · 1:500 dilution", mix 1 part product to 500 parts water. For 20 L water, use about 40 mL of product.

应用显示"叶面 · 1:500 倍稀释"时，1 份产品配 500 份水。20 升水加入约 40 毫升产品。

③ Rotation matters · 轮换很重要

In stages with "Rotation 1/3, 2/3, 3/3", use a different product each month. Don't apply all three in the same month.

显示"轮换 1/3, 2/3, 3/3"的阶段，每月施用不同产品。请勿同月同时施用三种。

④ Watering during Pre-Flowering · 催花期间停水

Stop irrigation during the Pre-Flowering stage. Water stress helps trigger flowering. Resume normal watering after Fruit Set.

催花阶段停止灌溉。水分胁迫有助诱导开花。坐果后恢复正常灌溉。

⑤ When in doubt, ask · 不确定时请询问

This planner is a guide. Trees are living things — if something looks unusual (yellowing leaves, no flowers, fruit drop), ask your supervisor or agronomist before changing the plan.

本规划器仅供参考。树是活的——如出现异常（叶黄、不开花、落果），请先咨询主管或农艺师再调整方案。

7Problems? 遇到问题？

The app won't open · 应用无法打开

If you have no internet on the very first time, the app can't load. Connect to Wi-Fi once, open the app, then it will work offline after that.

首次打开需联网。连接 Wi-Fi 后打开一次，之后即可离线使用。

The page looks broken · 页面显示异常

Close the app fully and reopen it. If still broken, uninstall (long-press the icon → Remove) and reinstall using the steps in Section 1.

完全关闭应用后重新打开。如仍异常，卸载（长按图标→移除）后按第 1 节重新安装。

Doses look wrong · 用量看起来不对

Check your Tree Age selection at the top. Doses scale automatically — if you picked the wrong age band, the numbers will be off. The app uses the 10+ year dose as the baseline and scales down for younger trees.

请检查顶部的树龄选择。用量按树龄自动换算——选错树龄会导致数值不对。应用以 10 年以上树为基准，幼树按比例缩减。

The language is mixed · 语言混合显示

That's by design. The app shows English and Chinese side-by-side so the whole team can read it.

这是设计如此。应用同时显示中英文，方便整个团队阅读。
